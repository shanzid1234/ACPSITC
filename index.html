About Us

Adamjee Cantonment Public School is a distinguished educational institution. Alongside imparting education, this institution runs a number of different clubs with a view to flourishing the latent of the students and IT Club is one of them. The members of this club are skilled and bold enough to run all activities of the club. The members of the IT Club participate in ICT Competitions in different schools and colleges and put a mark of success in their respective fields. Taking part in different events like IT Olympiad, Gaming, Multimedia Presentation Projects and many other events they are securing first positions and earning fame for the school. The members of this club arrange conferences among themselves on every Saturday of each month to chalk out development plans for the club. This is how this club is gradually thriving towards farther development.
